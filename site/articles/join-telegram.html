Join the Community on Telegram

Our community Telegram hub began with the merging of five highly active WhatsApp groups. Today, we stand as a thriving network of over 1,300 active members, making it the premier real-time communication platform for the Pittsburgh Indian diaspora.

What We Discuss

We boast an open and supportive environment. While sharing is broadly allowed, we gently enforce relevance to the Pittsburgh region or desi culture in general. From professional event promotions and local business updates to urgent requests for flatmates and moving sales, this group serves as the city's living bulletin board.

How to Join

If you already have the Telegram app installed on your smartphone, you can instantly join the channel by clicking the specialized invite link below. Our administrators work hard to moderate the group and keep it free from spam.

Join Pittsburgh Telegram →

A Note on Website Listings

You may occasionally see active Telegram postings cross-listed under the 'User Posts' directory on our website. To ensure maximum privacy, our administration team strips all direct contact information and only lists a minimal identifier (e.g., `@TJK`). You must join the secured Telegram group to look up the username and initiate a direct message concerning the listing.
