Final Rating

Arrey bhai, what a movie this is. This is not trying to be subtle or realistic. It arrives with drums, slow motion, flying dust, and full confidence that the hero can solve most problems by staring harder than everyone else.

🕴 Story

The movie keeps things very simple: one dangerous man returns, villains start trembling, and the audience sits back waiting for the payoff.

Enough is enough.

Logic is not the main guest here. Presentation is the star, and the film knows exactly where to spend its energy.
